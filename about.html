The people

Created by Sophie True, Alex Jordan, and Thomas Jamrozik for a 9th grade Health class in which our final project for the trimester was to create a Public Service Announcement, or PSA.

We chose to create a website about this issue because we feel that it doesn't get enough attention. People just don't understand that texting while driving is an issue.

Alex Jordan

Alex has a love of code and reading. He is a vocal supporter of anti-censorship movements, open source, and software freedom, and he designed and coded the entirety of the website.

Sophie True

Sophie is super gullible and loves basketball. Other than that, she spearheaded the social efforts of this project, creating the Facebook page, Twitter account, and Gmail account.

Thomas Jamrozik

Thomas is just a guy. He's super ordinary, but he researched and wrote almost all of the textual content on the Educate Yourself page, for which we are all grateful.
